Football Video Analytics

Football Video Analytics is a robust system that leverages advanced computer vision and AI to track player movements, measure performance metrics, and automatically produce commentary for uploaded match footage. Powered by GPT’s API for on-the-fly text generation and enhanced with ElevenLabs’ natural-sounding voice output, this solution delivers real-time insights and engaging commentary for coaches, scouts, and football enthusiasts.

Simply upload a match video, and the platform uses computer vision techniques to detect players, identify key events, and compile highlights. GPT dynamically generates meaningful, context-aware analysis, while ElevenLabs seamlessly converts this text into lifelike audio commentary. Whether you’re evaluating tactics, pinpointing standout plays, or sharing highlights with a broader audience, Football Video Analytics streamlines every step—bringing a new level of immersion and intelligence to post-match reviews.
