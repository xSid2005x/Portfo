AgroPulse: IoT Farm Monitoring & Automation

AgroPulse is an innovative IoT-based system designed to revolutionize farm management by providing real-time monitoring and automation. The system integrates various IoT sensors to collect data on soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and other critical parameters. This data is then analyzed to optimize irrigation, enhance crop yield, and reduce resource wastage. AgroPulse offers farmers a comprehensive dashboard with actionable insights, enabling smarter decision-making and efficient resource management.
